Bringing Millbrook Together Since 1873

A Gathering Place for Generations

Located in the center of Millbrook, Ashwood Park has been a cherished community space for over 150 years. From summer picnics to community celebrations, this park has been a constant backdrop to our town's shared memories.

Community Happenings

Throughout the year, Ashwood Park hosts a variety of events that bring our community together. From the annual summer concert series to weekend farmers markets, there's always something happening at the park.

Park Hours: Sunrise to Sunset

Open to all Millbrook residents and visitors

A Space for Everyone

Ashwood Park is more than just green space—it's a place where neighbors meet, children play, and community bonds grow stronger. Whether you're looking for a quiet spot to read or a place to enjoy local festivities, our park welcomes you.
